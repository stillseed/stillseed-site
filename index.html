About Your Teacher

Why Depth Matters

Most mindfulness today is a quick fix — an app, a weekend certificate, a trend. Stillseed is different because it is rooted in sustained practice.

I have spent over ten years studying Focus and Insight meditation with Buddhist teachers across traditions. I practice daily. I still sit. I still learn. This is not a credential — it is a way of life that I share because it has changed mine.

The coffee ritual, the breathwork, the retreats — all of it comes from direct experience. When you learn from someone who still practices, you feel the difference immediately.

Orgyen Thupten Dhargye

(Mark Low) · Founder of Stillseed

Training: 10+ years of Buddhist meditation practice across multiple traditions. Trained in classical Focus and Insight meditation techniques under recognized teachers.

Teaching: Private sessions, group classes, weekend retreats, and corporate programs in Klang Valley and online. Sessions available in English.

Approach: Combines classical meditation with embodied daily practices — mindful coffee brewing, conscious breathwork, and sensory awareness. Designed for modern professionals who need depth without leaving their lives.

Location: Based in Klang Valley, Malaysia. In-person sessions available locally. Online sessions available worldwide.
